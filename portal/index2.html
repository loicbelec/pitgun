Why Pitgun?

Distributed Compute

Offload heavy Monte Carlo simulations to the edge. Pitgun Solver compiles to WebAssembly, allowing browser-based volunteer computing grids to process thousands of scenarios in parallel.

Type-Safe & Signed

Built on Rust's type system with a strict IDL. Configuration manifests and tuning policies are cryptographically signed by the Authority, ensuring that every simulation result is traceable and authentic.

Modular Design

Composable crates. Use pitgun-core for local formula evaluation, deploy pitgun-gateway for protocol normalization, or spin up the full mesh. Designed for F1-grade reliability from day one.

The Stack
